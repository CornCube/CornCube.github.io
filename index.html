EarthLink Account Services

We're sorry to see you go! If you wish to delete your EarthLink account, please send us an email with your request. Our team will process your request and ensure all your data is securely removed from our servers.

How to Request Account Deletion:

  1. Compose an email to manas.s.malla@vanderbilt.edu.
  2. Include your EarthLink account details (username/email).
  3. Specify “Account Deletion Request” in the subject line.
  4. Send the email, and our team will confirm once your account is deleted.

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to contact us at the same email address. We're here to help!

Report a User

If you need to report another user for any reason, please send an email to manas.s.malla@vanderbilt.edu with the details of the issue. Our team will investigate and take appropriate action.
